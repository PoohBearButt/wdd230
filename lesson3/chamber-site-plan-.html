Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

The site will be called "Pengu Chamber of Commerce"

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to serve the city and general area of Pengu. The events and interests of the local businesses and organizations will be promoted to encourage people, business, collaborations, and tourism.

Scenarios

Parents

    To find fun activities to do with their kids

    To organize fun activities for their kids

    To organize activities or meetups with other parents.

Businesses

    To share promotional events

    To recruit

    To do fundraisers

    To promote their products and service

    To share their special deals and discounts

Organizations

    To create events to attract people to attend

    To share what they are up to

Tourists & Visitors

    For them to look at what events are happening and where they will be.

    For then to find things to do in the local area

Comment Sections

For locals and visitors to leave comments on their impressions and experience for others to look at and help the business and organizations gain more traffic.
